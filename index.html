About Me

An overview of who I am as a person

My name is Darian Salahi, and I’m a student at George Mason University pursuing a B.S. in Information Technology with a concentration in Networking and Telecommunications.

I am eager to apply my networking and programming skills in hands-on IT roles or internships.

I discovered my passion for computers during my senior year of high school, when a computer-networking elective revealed how naturally my love for solving puzzles translated into working with technology.

Through my 100- to 300-level IT courses, I’ve become comfortable with HTML, Python, SQL, and elements of Java, and I’ve learned how to rapidly apply that knowledge to create fun, hands-on projects. Some examples include this very website and my personal favorite — a self-built program that automates the creation of YouTube Shorts with a single click.

You can view my résumé below for more details on coursework and experience. I hope you enjoy exploring this page and learning more about my skills, hobbies, and ambitions.
